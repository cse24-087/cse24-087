About Manchester City Women

Manchester City Women's Football Club is a professional women's football club based in Manchester, England. Founded in 1988, the team has grown to become one of the most successful clubs in the FA Women's Super League.

The team plays its home matches at the Academy Stadium, sharing the City Football Academy training complex with the men's team. Under the leadership of manager Gareth Taylor, City Women have won multiple domestic trophies and regularly compete in the UEFA Women's Champions League.
