About Manchester City FC

Manchester City Football Club is a professional football club based in Manchester, England. The club was founded in 1880 as St. Mark’s (West Gorton), later becoming Manchester City in 1894. City’s home ground, the Etihad Stadium, is located in East Manchester, where they have played since 2003.

Over the years, Manchester City has risen to become one of the most successful football clubs in England, with numerous Premier League titles, FA Cups, and League Cups in their trophy cabinet. In recent years, the club has cemented itself as a powerhouse under the leadership of manager Pep Guardiola.

Known for their attacking style of play and success in both domestic and European competitions, City’s fan base continues to grow globally. The club's focus is not only on success on the pitch but also on supporting the community and fostering youth development.
